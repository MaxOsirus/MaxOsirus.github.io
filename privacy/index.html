Privacy Notice

P8Launcher is designed to respect your privacy and works with data stored locally on your device.

No account or sign-in is required. No analytics or advertising SDKs are included, and no tracking technologies are used. P8Launcher does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information.

Your carts, save data, save states, play history, favorites, genres, controller mappings, custom layouts, and other library information are stored locally on your device.

Access to the Files app and the Photos picker is only used when you choose to import cartridges or select custom cover images.

Spotlight indexing and Home Screen widgets use your local library data to provide on-device system integrations. This information is not sent to or collected by the developer.

When you choose to open a cartridge's BBS thread or other external links, P8Launcher opens your web browser, which then connects directly to those websites. Those websites operate under their own privacy policies.
